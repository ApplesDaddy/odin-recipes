French Fries

Image courtesy of https://www.recipetineats.com/wp-content/uploads/2022/09/Crispy-Fries_8.jpg

The Recipe

Delicious fried potatoes

Ingredients

  • 1kg russet potatoes
  • 1 tbsp salt
  • 1L canola oil
  • salt

Steps

  1. Gather your equipment and ingredients.
  2. Wash and dry potatoes thoroughly
  3. Preheat your oil in a deep pot over medium until it hits ~350F
  4. While the oil is preheating, cut your potatoes into your desired french fry shape
  5. Fry your potatoes until golden brown and delicious
  6. Receive your fries into a strainer placed on top of a bowl, and sprinkle over your seasoning while tossing
  7. Serve immediately
