Beef Stew

The Recipe

A hearty beef stew, perfect for the colder months

Ingredients

  • 2lb beef shank and/or chuck
  • 2 carrots
  • 1 medium onion
  • 20g fresh ginger
  • 4 cloves garlic
  • 1-2 stalks lemongrass
  • 2 tbsp oil
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 2 tbsp hoisin sauce
  • 2 tbsp tomato paste
  • 2-3 pods star anise
  • 3-4 bay leaves
  • 2 cups chicken broth
  • 1 can of coconut soda
  • salt
  • pepper

Steps

  1. Cut meat into stew-sized chunks
  2. Marinate the meat with soy sauce, hoisin sauce, and 1 tsp salt. Place in the fridge for 30 minutes.
  3. Chop your aromatics and vegetables
  4. Preheat your oil in a large pot
  5. Sear your beef
  6. Add in the aromatics and fry until fragrant
  7. Add in the tomato sauce and cook until the colour darkens slightly
  8. Deglaze with coconut soda
  9. Add chicken broth until beef is covered
  10. Bring to a boil, then lower heat until the stew is simmering. Check and adjust seasoning.
  11. Cook until beef is tender, about 50-75 minutes
  12. Add carrots and cook for 10 more minutes
  13. Serve with bread or mashed potatoes
