The Recipe

A delicious summer vegetable stew from the south of France, featured in Disney Pixar's movie "Ratatouille"

Ingredients

  • 4 tbsp olive oil
  • 4 garlic cloves
  • 1/2 small onion
  • 1/3 cup carrot
  • 420g crushed tomatoes
  • 2 tsp dried basil
  • 1/2 tsp dried parsley
  • 1 small eggplant
  • 2 small zucchinis
  • 3 roma tomatoes
  • salt
  • black pepper

Steps

  1. Gather your ingredients and cut them appropriately
  2. Preheat the oven
  3. Heat half of the olive oil over medium heat
  4. Saute onion, garlic and carrot
  5. Add crushed tomatoes and season to taste. Simmer for 15 minutes
  6. Once the sauce has thickened, receive into an oven-safe dish (if necessary)
  7. Arrange the sliced vegetables into a circular, alternating pattern
  8. Drizzle the other half of the olive oil on top
  9. Cover and bake for 30 minutes, then uncover and bake until vegetables are tender
  10. Serve immediately
